Welcome to the best eCommerce around.

CupCart is an eCommerce platform based on the ideaologies and features of the popular WordPress blogging software. It boasts many of the same features that make WordPress great - but tweaked and sometimes reimagined to produce the best eCommerce experience for users, developers and store owners.

Fully Open-Source

All of our code is open-source and available to everybody. We're also open to community involvement to help make CupCart better. The best part about being open-source, we believe, is it gives 3rd party developers the opportunities they need to build the best tools to enhance your store.

The Best eCommerce Tools

We haven't built you a control panel - no what we've built is a full management portal. From your CupCart dashboard you can oversee and administrate every facet of your online business from inventory and warehouse management to currency conversions and drop shipping.

Fully Customizable Themes

CupCart comes with a fancy style when you first install but you are free to choose any of our available themes to choose how your store should look. Don't like any of our themes? Build one. Just like anybody can build a WordPress theme anybody can build a CupCart theme.

Plugins

Use our plugins library to add functionality to your store. Shipping estimates, billing gateways and more are available to all CupCart users.

But wait there's more!

If all this isn't enough already throw in widget support, affiliate management, easy to edit web pages and so much more we can't list it all here! You should just install CupCart and see what it can do.
